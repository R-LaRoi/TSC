mission
Trupti Samuel consultancy offers a variety of services that is designed to support children and families from a holistic lens. Interventions are based on attachment, child development, trauma informed practises; and the likes.

The goal is to provide a safe and comfortable space to be heard and thereby guided from a place of curiosity and hope, to provide practical support.

SERVICES

If you are seeking support to better manage your child's behaviours; I can offer practical guidance. I have over a decade of experience working with children and families. I offer three main services. For more information to see if I am the right person to support your family, schedule an appointment below.

Curious Package
Professional Development for Early Childhood Educators
The curious package focuses on professional development - If you're seeking professional development workshops for early childhood and primary school educators; I design sessions based on educator's needs. My workshops have focused on introduction to trauma, enhancing emotional and social developments, and the likes.
Connection Package
Parenting Consults
The connection package is for parents experiencing challenging behaviours with their children. Evidence based strategies are provided to support parents manage children's behaviours.
Calm Package
Baby Massage (B.M)
The calm package focuses on baby massage (BM) – I can provide massage techniques to help parents soothe their babies. BM helps with stimulation, increased sleep, muscle development; and can further help parents develop a closer connection with their babies by reading their cues and needs.
